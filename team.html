Managing Director

Promise Buthelezi

He started his career in 2010 when he did his articles at Auditor General (SA). He has held different managerial roles until he was a senior audit manager for major public entities and departments. He obtained experience in the local government, public entities, financial services and broadcasting. He was also responsible for appraising parliamentary committees and forums on the status of internal controls of government institutions.

He also held a financial reporting specialist, where he was responsible for overall financial and management accounting. This role entailed working closely with the Governance structures through reporting. It incorporated preparation of annual financial statements (incl. tax calculations), co-ordination of the audit process, budgeting & forecasting, performance information reporting, drafting action plans on external & internal audit findings, technical training and monthly & quarterly reporting of management accounts.

1 Company story

We are a young and dynamic firm thriving through innovation and creative – thinking. We have vast experience in the public sector and small medium entity sector.
We are affiliated to South African Institute of Chartered Accountants which ensures optimum technical proficiency is upheld.

We have been in existence since year 2017, however the founder has an experience extending to over 8 years in service. In this period in-depth experience was gained in external & internal audit, annual financial statements preparation and audit support, management accounting, training, taxation calculations & audits, internal control reviews, governance framework reviews, compliance as well as performance audits.
